How We Started

From a small beginning to a big impact, discover the inspiring journey of Nasha Ministries.

Our Humble Beginnings

Nasha Ministries started with a small group of volunteers who were passionate about making a difference in the lives of children. Our journey began with just a handful of kids and a big dream.

First Major Milestone

With the support of our community and dedicated donors, we were able to secure our first major funding which allowed us to expand our programs and reach more children in need.

Expansion and Growth

As our impact grew, so did our network of partners and supporters. We expanded our programs, introduced new initiatives, and continued to strive for greater impact in the community.

Current Day

Today, Nasha Ministries stands as a beacon of hope and change, having touched countless lives. We remain committed to our mission and are grateful for the support that has helped us reach this milestone.
