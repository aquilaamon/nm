Psalm 112:9

“ He has scattered abroad, he has given to the poor; his righteousness endures forever; his horn will be exalted in honor. ”
Psalm 112:9 emphasizes the importance of generosity towards the poor. It portrays those who give to the needy as righteous individuals who enjoy God's favor. Their charitable acts are seen as a reflection of their faith and trust in God's provision. This verse serves as a powerful reminder for Christians to prioritize the needs of others and to share their blessings with those less fortunate.
